About Me

I'm a graphic designer focused on branding and visual storytelling, with a background in Graphic Design, Marketing, and Mass Communication from Lehigh University. I'm based in the New Jersey / New York Metro area.

I'm especially interested in creating cohesive brand systems through print, packaging, layout, and other tangible design work. I enjoy building visual "universes" that help people and brands find their voice and express their stories in meaningful ways.

My goal is to create work that feels intentional, gets noticed, and improves the end user's experience, whether through function, mood, or a moment of visual delight. When I'm not designing, you can find me scrapbooking, traveling, or eating pasta!
